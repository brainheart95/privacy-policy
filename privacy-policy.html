Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-31

الكتاب المقدس بالعربي is a simple, offline Bible reading app with bookmarking, search, and substring highlighting.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information.

Local-Only Data

Bookmarks, highlights, and search history are stored locally on your device and are never sent to us or to third parties. Deleting the app will remove this locally stored data.

Internet & Permissions

  • The app does not require an account or sign-in.
  • The app does not request sensitive permissions (e.g., location, contacts, camera, microphone).
  • The app can be used fully offline.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Policy Changes

If we change this policy, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
