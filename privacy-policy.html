Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-26

الكتاب المقدس بالعربي is a simple, offline Bible reading app with bookmarking, search, and text highlighting features.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies of any kind.

Local-Only Data

Bookmarks, highlights, and search history are stored locally on your device and are never sent to us or to third parties. Deleting the app should remove this locally stored data.

Internet & Permissions

  • The app does not require an account or sign-in.
  • The app does not request sensitive permissions (e.g., location, contacts, camera, microphone).
  • The app can be used fully offline.

This app is designed to be appropriate for users of all ages. However, it is not specifically directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children. If a parent or guardian believes that a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
